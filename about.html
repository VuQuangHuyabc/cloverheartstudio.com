About Clover Heart Studio

Our story, our passion, our craft

Our Story

Clover Heart Studio was born from a passion for creating beautiful, meaningful jewelry that brings joy and positive energy to people's lives.

Founded in 2020, our small family-owned business is located in the heart of Santa Fe, New Mexico. The vibrant culture and artistic spirit of our city inspire every piece we create.

What started as a hobby quickly grew into a full-time passion as more people discovered our unique handmade bracelets. Each piece is crafted with love, attention to detail, and a commitment to quality that sets us apart.

We believe that jewelry should be more than just an accessory – it should be a reflection of your personality, a source of positive energy, and a reminder of the beauty in everyday life.

Our Craftsmanship

The art behind every bracelet

Each design starts with inspiration from nature, culture, and our customers' desires. We sketch and refine until we achieve the perfect balance of beauty and wearability.

We carefully select high-quality materials that meet our standards for beauty, durability, and sustainability. Each component is chosen with intention.

Before any piece leaves our studio, it undergoes rigorous quality inspection to ensure it meets our high standards for craftsmanship and durability.

Our Santa Fe Studio

Located in the heart of Santa Fe, New Mexico, our studio is where the magic happens.

Our workshop at 1012 Marquez Place is more than just a workspace – it's a creative sanctuary where inspiration flows freely and beautiful jewelry comes to life.

The vibrant colors of the Southwest landscape, the rich cultural heritage of Santa Fe, and the supportive community of local artists all contribute to the unique character of our designs.

We welcome visitors by appointment to see our craft in action and to experience the passion that goes into every piece we create.
