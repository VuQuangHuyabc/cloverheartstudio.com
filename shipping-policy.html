Shipping Policy

Information about shipping and delivery

Order Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1-3 business days to ensure timely delivery for our customers.

Once your order is placed, our team carefully prepares your handmade bracelets with the attention to detail they deserve. Each piece undergoes quality inspection before being packaged for shipment.

Shipping Options Available

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days, with expedited options available at checkout for faster delivery.

Standard Shipping

Delivery Time: 5-7 business days

Cost: Free on orders over $50, otherwise $5.99

Perfect for most orders, our standard shipping provides reliable delivery with tracking information.

Expedited Shipping

Delivery Time: 2-3 business days

Cost: $12.99

Need your order faster? Choose expedited shipping for priority handling and delivery.

Express Shipping

Delivery Time: 1-2 business days

Cost: $19.99

Our fastest shipping option for when you need your handmade bracelets urgently.

Transparent Shipping Policies

We provide clear shipping policies to ensure transparency and customer satisfaction with every order.

Shipping Information

  • All orders are shipped from our studio in Santa Fe, New Mexico
  • Tracking information is provided for all orders
  • Signature may be required for orders over $100
  • We ship to all 50 states within the United States
  • International shipping is not currently available

Packaging

Each bracelet is carefully packaged in our signature Clover Heart Studio gift box, ensuring it arrives safely and beautifully presented. We use eco-friendly packaging materials whenever possible to minimize our environmental impact.

Shipping Delays

While we strive to meet our shipping timelines, please understand that during peak seasons (holidays, special promotions) or due to unforeseen circumstances (weather, carrier issues), there may be delays. We appreciate your patience and will keep you informed of any significant delays.

Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package is lost or arrives damaged, please contact us immediately at contact@cloverheartstudio.com. We will work with the shipping carrier to resolve the issue and ensure you receive your order or a replacement.

Special Offer

Get free shipping on all orders over $50! No code needed - the discount will be applied automatically at checkout.

Questions About Shipping?

If you have any questions about our shipping policies or need assistance with your order, please don't hesitate to contact us.
